Technopark Phase III

Technopark Phase II is a captive SEZ campus leased out to UST Global and Infosys. Out of 86 acres, 50 acres is leased out to Infosys and 36 acres to M/s UST Technology International (P) Ltd for creating their own IT Infrastructure. Technopark Phase II campus is located near Kazhakkoottam.

Technocity

Technocity is a technology park that is an integrated township currently under construction in Trivandrum, India dedicated to electronics, software, and other information technology. It was conceived in 2005 as the fourth phase of development of Technopark.

Technopark Kollam

Technopark Phase V (Kollam) spans an area of 4.44 acres of land and features a LEED Gold-certified building known as Ashtamudi. This remarkable building covers 1 lakh sq ft and is set against the enchanting backdrop of the spectacular Ashtamudi Lake, with its vast expanse of pristine waters.
